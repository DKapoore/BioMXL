How to Use Bio MXL

Simple, precise, and highly effective application methods for maximum results

500

3
ml / L water

1

Soil Preparation

Mix 500ml Bio MXL with 50L water. Apply evenly across one acre before sowing or transplanting. Best done 3–5 days before seeding.

2

Seed Treatment

Soak seeds in 3ml Bio MXL per litre of water for 30 minutes before sowing. This gives seedlings an early-life advantage from day one.

3

Vegetative Stage

Apply 250ml per acre via drip or 500ml via flood irrigation at 20–25 days after germination for accelerated vegetative growth.

4

Flowering & Fruiting

Foliar spray of 3ml per litre at the flowering stage significantly increases fruit set, grain filling, and final yield.
